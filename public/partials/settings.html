History

ANGTFT was built by four Computer Science students at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign as part of their CS498: The Art and Science of Web Development. All four students realized that remedial tasks were not worth the time and effort. Inspired by websites such as Ebay and Craigslist, the four students decided to make a website that would allow users to post and bid on errands.

How to Use

ANGTFT is extremely user friendly. Simply sign up and will be allowed to post errands and bid on the errands posted by other users. You may also view your errand history through your profile page. Transactions must be done in person and ANGTFT is not responsible for providing or facilitating payment.

If you are busy and want others to do your remedial tasks, post them on here. You'll be amazed by the number of people in your area willing to perform your tasks for you!

Looking to make a few easy bucks. Bid on tasks and complete them to be rewarded.
